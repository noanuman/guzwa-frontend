# GUZWA — Branding Guidelines

## Identity
**Name:** GUZWA
**Tagline:** Smarter commutes, less congestion
**Feel:** Modern, trustworthy, urban, approachable

## Color Palette

| Role | Hex | RGB | Usage |
|------|-----|-----|-------|
| Primary Dark | `#3852B4` | `rgb(56, 82, 180)` | Headers, primary buttons, nav bar |
| Primary Light | `#5E7AC4` | `rgb(94, 122, 196)` | Hover states, secondary elements, links |
| Accent Warm | `#F3BE7A` | `rgb(243, 190, 122)` | Highlights, badges, notifications |
| Accent Bold | `#F08D39` | `rgb(240, 141, 57)` | CTAs, alerts, active indicators |

### Usage Rules
- **Primary pair** (`#3852B4` / `#5E7AC4`) — all navigation, structure, trust elements
- **Accent pair** (`#F3BE7A` / `#F08D39`) — attention, action, warmth
- Don't use accents for large surfaces — reserve for emphasis
- Ensure WCAG AA contrast on text (use white text on Primary Dark, dark text on Accent Warm)

## Typography
- Use system font stack or a clean sans-serif (Inter, Plus Jakarta Sans)
- Headings: semibold/bold, Primary Dark color
- Body: regular weight, neutral dark (`#1A1A2E` or similar)

## Tone
- Friendly but efficient — users are commuters, respect their time
- Action-oriented copy ("Find a ride", "Share your spot")
- Avoid jargon — keep it accessible
